
And put it in a disk image file: 
"""
qemu-img create -f raw hda.img 1G
mkfs.fat hda.img
sudo mount -o uid=$UID hda.img /mnt
mkdir -p /mnt/efi/boot/
mv bootIA32.efi /mnt/efi/boot/
sudo umount /mnt
"""
